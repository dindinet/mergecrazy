Tune your website for higher conversion

Don't drive your visitors into the waiting arms of your competitors

If your website is slow to load or dispays poorly on your visitors devices, especially smart phones it will hinder conversion. People dislike slow loading websites. If you make people wait, they will abandon their visit and probably go directly to one of your competitors. Search engines want to serve the best possible results to their visitors. Search engines do not favour slow loading sites. Equaly, if your website is difficult to navigate or does not display optimally on visitors preferred devices, they will bounce. Search engines do not reward website with a high number of bounces.

Learn more about tuning your website and reducing visitor bounce rate(tune headers, footers, contact, faq to be always closing)

Set up Analytics to measure your success

When you have the correct accurate data you can make the right decisions.

Know which sources are sending the most valuable visitors and where the timewasters are coming from. See which content is generating the most interest. Find out what visitors are doing on your web pages and what might be causing them to bounce. See what visitors click on. See at what point visitors drop off.
